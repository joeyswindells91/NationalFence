About Us

National Fence & Supply Co. Owner and Operator Richard Cathcart Has Been Involved in the Fence Industry Since 1969

National Fence & Supply Company was founded in the 1940s by Richard's father-in-law. When Richard eventually took over the helm of the business from his father-in-law, he recognized and honored the business values and civic pride his father-in-law galvanized during the formative years of the company. Profound pride in each and every fence installation, no matter how big or small, has amplified National Fence’s reputation throughout Massachusetts as a company that can be trusted.

Richard is personally fulfilled by working hand in hand with his customers. Gaining the gift of great satisfaction by installing fences for the grandchildren and sometimes great grandchildren of the company's first customers. His decades of experience as not only an owner, but an installer make National Fence a natural choice for your next fence install.

Richard is also a proud member of the Free Masons, and is actively involved state wide with the many altruistic services they offer to their various communities. He is proud of his involvement within this fellowship. This mirrors the National Fence ideology of championing the notion of doing the right thing in the right way for all members of the community. It doesn’t matter if you are the big guy or the small guy, your fence installation is in good hands with Richard and his team at National Fence & Supply Company.
